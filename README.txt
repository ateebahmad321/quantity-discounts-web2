== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Will it work with my theme? =

We’ve tested our plugin with a variety of free and premium themes. As long as your theme is compatible with WooCommerce, it should work. If your theme doesn’t work with our plugin, don’t hesitate to reach out. We’re happy to help!

= Is it compatible with other plugins? =

We have tested BulkBoost & Breaks plugin with many third-party plugins and themes.

= Is it possible to use with variable products? =

Yes!

= What are the key features of the BulkBoost & Breaks plugin? =

The plugin offers dynamic quantity breaks, customizable bundle designs, real-time previews, and a range of settings to manage the appearance of discounts and pricing. It's designed to enhance your WooCommerce store's profitability through strategic bulk discount pricing.

= How can I set up quantity breaks in my WooCommerce store? =

Setting up quantity breaks is straightforward with our plugin. You can define quantity thresholds and corresponding discounts for each product directly from the product editing page in WooCommerce. For a step-by-step guide, check out our tutorial videos provided in the plugin description.

= Can I use this plugin to apply bundle discounts? =

Yes, the BulkBoost & Breaks plugin allows you to create customized bundles with specific discounts. You can set these discounts to trigger as customers increase the quantity of items in their carts, effectively using dynamic pricing to encourage larger purchases.

= How does the plugin handle WooCommerce dynamic pricing? =

The plugin employs a flexible pricing mechanism that adjusts product prices in real-time based on the quantity the customer is purchasing. This dynamic pricing strategy is ideal for stores looking to incentivize bulk purchases and increase average order value (AOV).

= What should I do if the plugin doesn't work with my theme? =

While the plugin is compatible with most WooCommerce themes, if you encounter any issues, please contact our support team. We offer priority customer support and are dedicated to resolving compatibility issues promptly to ensure your store operates smoothly.
